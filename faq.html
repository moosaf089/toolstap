Common Questions

Get quick answers to the most frequently asked questions about ToolsTap

What is ToolsTap?

ToolsTap offers a comprehensive collection of free online tools to help you convert, compress, and edit files — from images to documents — quickly and securely. All processing happens directly in your browser for maximum privacy.

Is ToolsTap really free to use?

Yes. Every tool on ToolsTap is completely free to use with no hidden costs. No sign-up, registration, or payment is ever required. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.

Do you store uploaded files?

No. Your privacy is our priority. Uploaded files are processed temporarily in your browser and automatically deleted once the process is completed. We don't store any files on our servers to ensure your data safety.

Does ToolsTap work on mobile devices?

Yes. ToolsTap is designed to be fully responsive, ensuring smooth usage across all mobile devices, tablets, and desktop browsers. Our tools are optimized for touch interfaces and smaller screens.

How can I contact ToolsTap support?

You can email us anytime at moosafaisal880@gmail.com or use the contact form available on the homepage. We typically respond within 24 hours and welcome your feedback and suggestions.

Are there any file size limitations?

Most tools have generous file size limits that accommodate typical usage. Specific limits vary by tool type, but we've designed them to handle most common file sizes without compromising performance.

What file formats do you support?

We support a wide range of popular file formats including PDF, DOCX, JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP for images, and various document formats. Each tool page specifies the supported formats for that particular utility.
