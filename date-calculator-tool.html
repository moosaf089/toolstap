How to Use

  • For "Days Between Dates": Select a start date and end date, then click "Calculate Days" to see the number of days between them.
  • For "Add/Subtract Days": Select a base date and enter the number of days to add (positive number) or subtract (negative number).
  • All calculations are performed instantly in your browser - no data is sent to any server.
  • Results are displayed immediately after clicking the calculate button.
  • You can use negative numbers in the "Days to Add/Subtract" field to calculate dates in the past.
