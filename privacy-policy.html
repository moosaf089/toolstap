Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and handle your data.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At ToolsTap, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect non-personal data such as browser type, tool usage, and device information to improve our services. We do not collect sensitive personal data.

Note: We do not store any personal information unless explicitly provided by you for specific tool functionality.

Types of Data Collected

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our tools and website
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and IP address for analytics
  • Tool-Specific Data: Files and content you process through our tools (processed locally when possible)

Use of Information

We use collected data to enhance user experience, monitor website performance, and maintain security.

How We Use Your Data

  • Improve our tools and user interface based on usage patterns
  • Monitor and analyze website performance and security
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Develop new features and tools that meet user needs

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies for analytics and improving user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website

Data Protection

We take necessary security measures to protect your data. However, no online platform can guarantee 100% security.

Our Security Measures

  • Limited data collection and retention policies
  • Secure server infrastructure

Third-Party Integrations
