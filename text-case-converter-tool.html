About Text Case Converter

A Text Case Converter is an essential tool for writers, editors, students, and professionals who work with text. It allows you to quickly transform text between different capitalization styles:

  • UPPERCASE - Converts all letters to capital letters
  • lowercase - Converts all letters to small letters
  • Title Case - Capitalizes the first letter of each major word
  • Sentence Case - Capitalizes only the first letter of each sentence

This free online tool processes your text instantly in the browser, ensuring your data remains private and secure. No registration required - just paste your text and convert!
