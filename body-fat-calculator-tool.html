How to Use

  • Basic Method: Uses age, height, weight, and waist circumference for a general estimate of body fat percentage.
  • U.S. Navy Method: Uses height, neck, waist, and hip (for females) measurements for a more accurate calculation.
  • BMI-based Estimate: Uses Body Mass Index to provide an approximate body fat percentage range.
  • For best results, take measurements in the morning before eating or drinking.
  • All calculations are estimates - for precise measurements, consult a healthcare professional.
  • Results are displayed with category classification based on standard body fat ranges.
